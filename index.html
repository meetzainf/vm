About Us

Tagta was created with the belief that small, thoughtful gestures can create meaningful connections. In a fast‑paced digital world, we wanted to bring back the warmth and impact of a carefully chosen greeting card.

Our cards combine modern design with heartfelt messages to help people celebrate, encourage, comfort, and connect. Whether it’s a birthday, a personal milestone, or a moment of self‑care, Tagta cards are made to feel intentional, uplifting, and personal.

At Tagta, we value authenticity, creativity, and positivity. Every card is designed with care to help people express emotions in a way that feels honest, memorable, and human.

Store Policies

Shipping

Physical cards are printed on demand and ship within 3–5 business days. Standard shipping typically arrives within 5–10 business days. Digital downloads are delivered instantly after purchase.

Returns & Refunds

Returns are accepted within 14 days for damaged or misprinted physical items. Digital downloads are non‑refundable due to instant delivery. Please contact us within 7 days if there is an issue with your order.
